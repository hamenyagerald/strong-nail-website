1. Executive Summary

Strong Nail Plumbing & Construction Ltd. seeks USD 15,000 (UGX 55,921,000) to supply water tanks & sanitation installations, expand plumbing services, and train youth plumbers. Project supports SDG 6: Clean Water & Sanitation.

2. Problem Statement

  • Over 38% of households lack access to safe water systems.
  • Poor sanitation contributes to cholera, diarrhea, typhoid.
  • Many families cannot afford quality plumbing or sanitation solutions.

3. Our Solution

  • Water Supply Systems – Installation of tanks, pipes, taps, drainage.
  • Sanitation Facilities – Toilets, bathrooms, septic tank connections, hygiene facilities.
  • Emergency Plumbing Services – Burst pipe repairs, leak detection, water-saving systems.
  • Community Training – Capacity building for youth plumbers and technicians.

4. Expected Impact

  • Install safe water and sanitation systems for 500 households.
  • Train and employ at least 10 young plumbers.
  • Reduce waterborne diseases in targeted communities by 20%.
  • Support schools, clinics, and local businesses with improved sanitation.
